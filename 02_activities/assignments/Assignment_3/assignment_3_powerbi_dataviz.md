Title : "Distribution of Audible Pedestrian Signals by Signal System"
    > What software did you use to create your data visualization?
    Power BI Desktop

    > Who is your intended audience? 
    This visualization is designed for city transportation planners and engineers in assessing the distribution and equity of Audible Pedestrian Signals (APS) in Toronto’s traffic signal systems.
    
    > What information or message are you trying to convey with your visualization? 
    The visualization highlights how many traffic signals in each signal system category (e.g., coordinated vs. non-system) are equipped with Audible Pedestrian Signals (APS).
    
    > What aspects of design did you consider when making your visualization? How did you apply them? With what elements of your plots? 
    I used an interactive bar graph with distinct colors representing each control mode. Power BI’s default tooltip and filter functionalities were retained to allow viewers to explore subgroups dynamically.
    
    > How did you ensure that your data visualizations are reproducible? If the tool you used to make your data visualization is not reproducible, how will this impact your data visualization? 
    The visual can be reproduced using the original dataset and steps I followed in Power BI (import, clean, filter, and visualize). While Power BI visuals are not easily exported as editable charts for others, the .pbix file can be shared so others can open, verify, or adjust the visualization in Power BI Desktop.
    
    > How did you ensure that your data visualization is accessible?  
    A colorblind-friendly palette was selected and included descriptive title and axis labels. 
    
    > Who are the individuals and communities who might be impacted by your visualization?  
    City transportation planners may use this to identify underserved areas.
    
    > How did you choose which features of your chosen dataset to include or exclude from your visualization? 
     I focused on just two columns:
    AUDIBLEPEDSIGNAL (presence/absence of APS)
    SIGNALSYSTEM (classification of traffic signals)

    > What ‘underwater labour’ contributed to your final data visualization product?
    Data cleaning included handling missing values, and transforming variables. I also spent time learning the tool, navigating Power Bi's Interface.
